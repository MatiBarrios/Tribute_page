Ing. Nikola Tesla

The man who changed the world

Here's a time line of Testa's life:

  • 1856 - Born in Semiljan, Croacia
  • 1870 - finished his elementary education a year early due to his academic performance, his mathematics and physics teacher Martin Sekulic had a great influence on him.
  • 1873 - Tesla contracted cholera, which left him bedridden for 9 months and on several occasions on the verge of death.
  • 1882 - Began working at Continental Edison Company.
  • 1884 - He arrived in New York and began working for the Edison Company.
  • 1885 - Tesla founded his own company Tesla Electrict Light and Manufacturing.
  • 1889 - Tesla created the first alternating current motor.
  • 1890 - made discoveries about flourecent and neon light.
  • 1898 - He publicly demonstrated his "automaton" technology by controlling a wireless boat.
  • 1903 - He began conducting experiments at Wardenclyffe in which he intended to transmit power wirelessly to the entire planet..
  • 1930 - He succeeded in creating the "peace ray" also called the "death ray" capable of shooting down enemy aircraft at great distances.
  • 1946 - dies at the age of 86 alone in the Wyndham hotel.
